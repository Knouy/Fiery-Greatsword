Banner of Discipline

Banner. Place a banner that improves precision and ferocity for you and nearby allies. Picking up the banner reduces the recharge of this skill.

Banner of Discipline: 100 Precision, 100 Ferocity
Recharge Time Reduced: 60 seconds
Number of Allied Targets: 10
Duration: 60 seconds
Effective Radius: 600
Combo Finisher: Blast
Range: 600
