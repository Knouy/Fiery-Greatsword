Welcome to Fiery Greatsword.

  • Designed for Fractals of the Mists, the precision calculator allows you to optimize your chances of critical hits without exceeding a precision threshold. It takes into account:

    • Agony Impedance
    • Attributes
    • Boons
    • Conditions
    • Consumables
    • Effects
    • Equipment
    • Fractal Attunements
    • Gizmos
    • Mist Attunements
    • Professions
    • Sigils
    • Traits
    • Utility Skills

  • The UCE counter calculates, using an API key, the number of unstable cosmic essences of an account.
