Agony Resistance

Agony Resistance is the attribute that reduces the damage taken from Agony, an effect exclusively found in the Fractals of the Mists which reduces healing and causes a large amount of damage to players without a sufficient amount of agony resistance.

Acquisition
• Infusions
• Anguished Tear of Alba
• Rigorous Certainty
• Agony Impedance (1, 2, 3, 4)
• Mist Attunement (1, 2, 3, 4)
